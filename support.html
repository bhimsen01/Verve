Frequently asked questons.

How do I become a member?
Sign up via the Sign Up page on our website and you can start joining events immediately. Membership is free for all students!
Is there a membership fee?
No, membership is completely free for all students of the college. Some specialized workshops may have material fees, but general events are free.
How do Admin privileges work?
Only users with the admin role can access the Admin Panel. Demo admin: admin@ars.edu. Admins can create events, manage workshops, and oversee member activities.
How often are workshops organized?
We organize workshops every week, covering various art forms and recreational activities. Check our events calendar for the latest schedule.
Do I need to sign up for workshops in advance?
Yes, most workshops require advance registration as spaces are limited. You can register through your member portal on our website.
Where can I find information about upcoming events?
All upcoming events are listed on our website's events calendar. You can also follow us on social media for regular updates.
Can I suggest an event or workshop?
Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from our members. Please use the "Contact Us" form on our website to share your ideas.
Are events open to non-members?
Most events are exclusive to members, but we occasionally host open events that everyone can attend. Check individual event details for information.
